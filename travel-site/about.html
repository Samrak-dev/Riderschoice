About us

Local hosts who care about comfort & safety

We are a small local business run by friendly hosts. Our goal is simple: help travellers explore freely on a scooty and return to a peaceful place to stay.

Why travellers choose us

  • Hospitality: Warm welcome and clear communication
  • Safety: Helmet included, basic guidance, fair rules
  • Cleanliness: Clean rooms and tidy spaces
  • Local guidance: Scenic routes, food spots, and timings

Our promise

We keep pricing transparent, handovers smooth, and support fast on WhatsApp. If you are visiting a coastal / hill / heritage destination, we’ll help you plan a relaxed schedule without rushing.

Need help choosing places to visit?

Message us your trip dates. We’ll suggest a simple plan for 1–3 days.
